Fellowship

Program Info

It is a 2-years long fellowship that starts with a 12-weeks immersive web development course and classes which run from 9am-4pm daily, 5 days a week. The training are anchored by specialized trainers and mentors to train, guide and answer any question of the new developers.


The next 3-months Following the in-class training our graduate developers will be hired by our partner companies for 3-months internships while some for immediate full-time employments. The new developers can now be recommended by their respective mentors for various positions and job placement that bring them to an expert developer state within the 2-years of their fellowships.
